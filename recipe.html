Creamy Chocolate Fudge

Description

The creamy chocolate fudge is the most delicious treat available on the surface of earth but it is not given much attention for no reason. Its one of the most underrated treats!

Greatest fudge of all times

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Coco Powder
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Vanilla Syrup

Steps

  1. In a bowl, break all the eggs.
  2. After that add some milk to the eggs and mix well.
  3. Then add some coco powder and make a dough like consistency.
  4. Add vanilla for flavour.
  5. Chill in the freezer for 10 minutes and enjoy!
